The Cartesian Political Chart


There are three algorithms used:

  1. Axis A: The comparisons are all left vs all right data, all authoritarian vs all libertarian tests. Normalized by total conditions found.
  2. Axis B: The comparisons are all left vs all right data, all authoritarian vs all libertarian tests. Normalized conditions by axis.
  3. Quadrant: The comparisons are testing data seperated by quadrant 1, 2, 3, 4. Normalized by total conditions found.
